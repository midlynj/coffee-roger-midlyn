Light City

Lightly roasted coffee can be clean, bright, and full of delicate floral and fruit notes with great acidity. They'll have a lighter body. It's in light roasts that the natural characteristics of the coffee can be tasted.

American

Americano is a bit stronger with a rich bold flavor. Americano typically will have a richer aroma than regular coffee. When you sit in front of our hot Americano, the wonderful aroma of a perfect espresso will waft through the air.

New Orleans

A cafe au lait is rich, dark, hot coffee that's mixed with almost-boiled milk. This comforting drink is enjoyed from morning to midnight by New Orleanians, often enticingly paired with hot, chewy beignets dusted with powdered sugar.

Continental

The Continental Blend is a blend of dark and light roasted arabica coffee beans - it has a very rich cup. Like the Breakfast Blend there are no roasting options to select. The coffees are roasted after ordering to a pre-determined leve and combined to form the Continental Blend.
